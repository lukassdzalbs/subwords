About Us

Subwords was created and is owned by Elitsia AB, a Swedish company based in Gothenburg. The game was launched in December 2021 and in 2022 the plan is to release two other apps, a quiz game and a global dating app.

We hope you like our game and we appreciate any feedback you would like to share so that we know how we can improve and develop the app further.

Good luck to beat your friends in multiplayer mode and we also hope you will be one of the few players who are able to pass all of the 100 levels.
